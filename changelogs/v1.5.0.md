# v1.5.0

The route planner gets more forgiving: undo any edit, grey out holds you're not sure about, and jump straight into a note's plan from the climb screen.

## What's new

**Route planner**
- **Undo** — a new undo button in the planner's bottom bar reverses your last edit: a placement, a drag, a removal, a group, or a reorder. Tap it again to keep stepping back. It's disabled when there's nothing left to undo.
- **Floating holds** — long-press a marker to "float" it, greying it out to flag an optional or uncommitted hold (a smear, a foot you might skip) without deleting it. Long-press again to restore it. Floating is just a visual note — it doesn't change your move order or playback.
- **Tidier bottom bar** — the **?** help button now sits with the play and move-sequence buttons, and the **Bubble size** label moved next to its +/− controls so the row reads more clearly.

**Notes**
- **Open a plan from a note** — tap a note on a climb's detail screen to jump straight into its plan. The note's text now shows along the bottom of the planner (scroll it if it's long) so your beta stays in view while you place moves.

## Fixed

- **Move numbers stay put** — the small sequence number on each marker no longer drifts off the bubble when the bubble size is turned down; it stays pinned to the marker at any size.

## Under the hood

- Schema **v5**: a `floating` flag (`INTEGER NOT NULL DEFAULT 0`) on `plan_moves`, threaded through `PlanMove`/`PlanMoveInput`, `DraftMove`, the query mapper, and `savePlanMoves`. Visual-only — sequence, grouping, and playback math are untouched. Existing plans migrate cleanly (older moves default to not-floating).
- New pure `toggleFloating` helper in `planSequence` (unit-tested, with a `toInputs` round-trip covering the field). `LimbMarker` gains a long-press gesture in its `Race` and a greyed render; `floating`/`onToggleFloating` flow through `PlanCanvas` → the planner screen.
- Undo is an in-memory snapshot stack (capped at 50) captured inside `commitMoves` and replayed through the existing persistence path; it resets when a plan (re)loads. `PlanEditBar` gains `onUndo`/`undoDisabled` and an `arrow-undo` button.
- `RouteCard` wires note taps to the note-scoped planner route; the planner loads the note body into its ready state and renders it in a scrollable blurb above the controls.
- The sequence badge is nested inside the dot instead of the larger touch box, so it stays anchored to the marker independent of bubble scale.
