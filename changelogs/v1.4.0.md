# v1.4.0

Notes get a big upgrade: keep several notes per climb, attach a photo or video to any of them, and plan specific moves right from a note. The planner gets a clearer start and friendlier markers.

## What's new

**Notes & beta**
- **Multiple notes per climb** — instead of one notes box, add as many notes as you like. Tap **Add Note**, type your beta, and optionally attach media.
- **Attach media to a note** — each note gets **Use Favorite** (reuse the climb's cover photo) and **Add Media** (Camera, Record Video, or Library — the same menu as the add button). A thumbnail appears above the note once media is attached.
- **Plan moves from a note** — attach a photo to a note and tap **Plan** to mark the limbs for that specific move. Planning is now about individual moves, not the whole climb. A **✓** in the planner's top-right takes you back to your note.
- **Rows or grid** — on a climb's detail screen, notes show as a list you can flip between **rows** (thumbnail + text) and **grid** (text over the photo). Notes without media show a placeholder.

**Route planner**
- **Set your start first** — a new plan now asks you to place all four limbs before free editing. It starts on your left hand, auto-advances to the next limb after each tap (left hand → right hand → left foot → right foot), and tells you how many are placed. Pick a different limb first if you like.
- **Clearer limb markers** — markers on the photo now use the actual hand/foot icons from the limb picker, with the move number shown as a small badge. The bubble-size control still resizes them.

The standalone "Plan route" button is gone — planning now lives inside your notes.

## Under the hood

- Schema **v4**: new `route_notes` table (`route_id`, nullable `media_id` → `route_media`, `body`, `position`) and a nullable `note_id` on `route_plans` so plans anchor to a note instead of the whole route. The legacy `routes.notes` column is kept and backfilled into one note per route. `RouteWithGym` gains `noteEntries: RouteNote[]`; `RouteInput` gains `noteEntries?: RouteNoteInput[]` (matched to gallery rows by URI at save time).
- Queries: `getNotesForRoute`, `getNoteById`, `persistRouteNotes` (id-preserving upsert that deletes dropped notes and their plans), and note-scoped `getPlanForNote`/`getOrCreatePlanForNote`. `usePlanStore.loadNotePlan` mirrors `loadPlan` for a single note.
- `RouteForm` holds notes as `NoteDraft[]` and persists-then-navigates to plan a note (`onPersistDraft`), reloading on focus so durable URIs and plan state sync back. New `NotesEditor`, `NotesSection`, and `MediaCaptureMenu` components; `notesLayout` ('rows' | 'grid') added to settings.
- Planner seeding: pure helpers `placedLimbs`, `isSeeding`, `nextSeedLimb` (unit-tested) drive the 4-limb start; grouping/play are disabled until the stance is complete. `LimbMarker` renders `LIMB_ICON` (left foot mirrored) with a numbered badge in both full and compact states; `PlanCanvas` passes `limb` instead of a text label.
- Tests + fixtures updated for `noteEntries` and `notesLayout`; new seeding tests in `planSequence`.
