# v1.2.0

Plan your beta. This release adds a full route planner: mark where each hand and foot goes on a photo of the climb, order the moves, and play the sequence back step by step.

## What's new

**Route planner**
- **Plan a route on the photo** — open a climb with a photo and tap **Plan route** to start mapping out your beta directly on the wall image.
- **Place hands and feet** — pick a limb (left hand, right hand, left foot, right foot) from the bottom bar, then tap the wall to drop it. Each tap adds the next move in your sequence.
- **Drag to fine-tune** — nudge any marker to line it up exactly with the hold.
- **Reorder and edit the sequence** — open the move list to see every move in order, move steps earlier or later, or delete them.
- **Step through your beta** — hit play and walk the route move by move; the limb that moves on each step glides to its next hold while the others stay put. Scrub back and forth with the slider.
- Plans are saved to the climb and survive restarts. The photo a plan is drawn on is copied into the app so your plan can't be lost.

**Logging**
- **Turn off the send-video prompt** — a new toggle in Settings lets you stop the "add a video?" prompt that appears when you mark a climb completed.

**Polish**
- **App icon** — It's A Rock now ships with a proper app icon (and an Android adaptive icon).

## Under the hood

- Enabled `react-native-gesture-handler` app-wide (`GestureHandlerRootView`) and used `react-native-reanimated` for marker dragging and playback animation.
- Added a normalized (0..1) coordinate system anchored to each photo's intrinsic pixels, so plans render correctly on any screen size or orientation; pure, unit-tested layout/coordinate helpers (`imageLayout`, `coords`).
- Schema migration v2 adds `route_plans` and `plan_moves` (ordered by `sequence`); playback state is derived from the move order, not stored.
- `persistPlanPhoto` copies a plan's photo into `documentDirectory` so cache eviction can't orphan a plan.
- New `usePlanStore` plus plan queries mirror the existing media persistence patterns; pure sequence/playback helpers (`planSequence`) are unit-tested.
- Added a persisted `promptSendVideo` setting honored by both the add/edit form and the list-completion flow.
