# v1.3.0

Plan real bouldering beta: mark several limbs moving at once as a single "frame" instead of one move at a time.

## What's new

**Route planner**
- **Group simultaneous moves into frames** — tap the new **Group** button (it lights up while on), then place the limbs that move together. They're saved as one frame, joined on the photo by a dotted line, so "both feet hop while the hands stay" is a single step. Tap **Group** again to go back to placing one limb at a time.
- **Frames in the move list** — grouped moves show as one bracketed block you can reorder and delete as a unit. You can also group a move with its neighbour, or pull a limb back out of a frame, right from the list.
- **Playback steps through frames** — scrubbing now advances one frame at a time and the caption names every limb that moves (e.g. "Move 3 of 5 — Left foot + Right foot"), with all of them highlighted on the wall.

Existing plans are unchanged — every move stays solo until you group it.

## Under the hood

- Schema **v3**: a nullable `group_id` on `plan_moves` (idempotent `ALTER TABLE`, guarded on `PRAGMA table_info`). Moves sharing a non-null id form a frame; null is solo. `PlanMove`/`PlanMoveInput`/`DraftMove` and `savePlanMoves`/`mapPlanMove` carry it through.
- New pure helpers in `planSequence.ts`: `framesOf`, `frameStanceAt`, `movingLimbsAt`, `reorderFrames`, `addToFrame`, `removeFromFrame`, `groupMoves`, and `nextGroupId`; `removeMove` now dissolves a frame left with one member. Frame members are kept contiguous so frames survive reorders and edits. A frame holds distinct limbs (membership edits that would duplicate a limb are rejected).
- The planner screen tracks a `groupId` for in-progress grouping; `PlanCanvas` draws dotted connectors between same-frame markers and supports multi-limb highlight (`CanvasMarker.highlighted`); `MoveList` renders frames with per-row membership controls; `PlaybackControls` takes `movingLimbs: Limb[]`.
- New unit tests cover the frame and membership helpers and frame-aware playback (`planSequence`, `planPlayback`).
