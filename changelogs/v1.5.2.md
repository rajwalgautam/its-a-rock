# v1.5.2

Add notes faster, plan just your hands, and edit a note or its plan straight from the climb card.

## What's new

**Climb card**
- **Add note button** — the climb detail card now has an **Add note** button, sitting just above your existing notes in the same style as **Edit**. Tap it to jump straight into the editor with a fresh, empty note ready to fill in.
- **Edit a note or its plan** — tapping a note on the climb card now offers a choice: **Edit note** to change its text, or **Edit plan** to open the planner for its photo.

**Route planner**
- **Hands-only plans** — when you attach a photo to a note, you can now choose **Hands Only** or **Full Plan**. Hands Only opens the planner with just the left- and right-hand markers, so beta that only tracks handholds no longer makes you place feet you don't care about.

## Under the hood

- `RouteCard` gained an **Add note** action styled like **Edit**; both route through a shared `startEditing(withNewNote)` helper that opens `RouteForm`. `RouteForm` takes a new optional `startWithNewNote` prop that appends one empty draft (via the now-exported `emptyDraft` from `NotesEditor`).
- New `NoteActionSheet` bottom sheet; tapping a note on the card opens it (**Edit note** → the edit form, **Edit plan** → the planner) instead of jumping straight to the plan.
- New `PlanMode` (`'hands' | 'full'`) with `limbsForMode`/`parsePlanMode` in `constants/plan`. The photo note's plan action offers **Hands Only** / **Full Plan** and passes the mode to the planner via a `mode` route param.
- `isSeeding`/`nextSeedLimb` now take an optional `limbs` list (defaulting to all four) so hands-only plans seed on just the two hands; `LimbSelector`/`PlanEditBar` accept a `limbs` prop and the planner threads `limbsForMode(mode)` through the seeding hint, selector, and placement flow. New unit tests cover hands-only seeding.
