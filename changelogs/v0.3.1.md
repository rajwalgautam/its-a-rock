# v0.3.1

A patch release fixing an immediate crash on startup in v0.3.0.

## What's new

**Bug fixes**
- Fixed app crash on startup caused by a version mismatch between `expo-file-system` v56 and the Expo 55 SDK. The app now correctly initializes the filesystem module and loads without crashing.

## Under the hood

- Downgraded `expo-file-system` from `^56.0.8` to `~55.0.14` to match the Expo 55 ecosystem. The incompatible version was causing a `NoSuchMethodError` when `FileSystemModule` tried to initialize, preventing the app from launching on physical devices.
