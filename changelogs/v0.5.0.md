# v0.5.0

A feature release focused on getting more out of your photos and grades, finding
climbs faster, and managing your gyms.

## What's new

**Photos**
- Tap a climb's photo in its detail view to open it full screen — double-tap to
  zoom in, drag to pan around, and save it to your gallery with the download button
- Crop and zoom your photo right after picking it from the library or taking it
  with the camera, so you can frame the boulder problem and trim the background

**Grades**
- Log a climb with a grade range like "V0 – V2" for problems that aren't marked
  with a single grade or that bridge difficulties — flip the new "Range" switch in
  the grade picker to choose a start and end grade
- Ranges show up everywhere single grades do — on tiles and in the detail card

**History: filter & sort**
- New "Filter & sort" controls on the History tab let you narrow climbs by
  completion (All / Sent / Projects), location, and grade range
- Sort by newest, oldest, hardest, easiest, or location
- A friendly empty state appears when no climbs match your filters, with one tap
  to clear them

**Locations**
- Manage your gyms and locations from Settings: add, rename, and delete them
- Deleting a location warns you first when it still has climbs logged at it
- Locations you add in Settings show up immediately in the climb form's picker

**Polish**
- The long-press menu on a tile now opens just below the tile (flipping above when
  there isn't room), so it no longer covers the climb you tapped

## Under the hood

- New `parseGradeRange` / `isValidGradeOrRange` grade utilities, with the range
  serialized as `V0-V2`; `formatGradeLabel` renders it as `V0 – V2`. No schema or
  data migration needed — single grades are unchanged.
- `GradePicker` refactored into a reusable single-grade `GradeScale` plus a range
  toggle that composes two scales.
- New pure `applyHistoryFilters` helper drives History filtering/sorting; filter
  state lives in the screen and resets on app restart.
- New `PhotoViewer` built on core React Native (`Animated` + `PanResponder`) so
  zoom/pan work on Android without the gesture-handler/reanimated runtime; the
  save button uses `expo-media-library` on demand only.
- New gym CRUD query functions (`createGym`, `updateGym`, `deleteGym`,
  `countRoutesForGym`) with cascade delete, surfaced through `useRouteStore` and a
  new `LocationsManager` settings section; a pure `validateGymName` helper guards
  empty/duplicate names.
- The tile long-press anchor now carries the tile's top and bottom edges so
  `RouteContextMenu` can position itself relative to the tile.
- Added unit tests for grade ranges, grade-label formatting, history filtering,
  and gym-name validation.
