# v1.3.1

A friendlier route-planner toolbar: bigger or smaller limb bubbles, clearer hand/foot buttons, and a roomier playback bar.

## What's new

**Route planner**
- **Resize the limb bubbles** — a new **Bubble size** control on the plan screen makes the dots on your photo bigger or smaller, so they cover the holds the way you like. Your choice is remembered across climbs.
- **Reworked controls** — the planning buttons now live in a tidy bottom bar: a large **Play** in the middle, **Group** on the left, and the move **sequence** on the right, with the limb picker right above them.
- **Hand and foot icons** — the limb picker now shows actual left/right hand and foot icons instead of "LH / RH / LF / RF" labels, so the limb you're placing is obvious at a glance.
- **Roomier playback** — the scrub slider and prev/next arrows no longer sit right at the bottom edge while a route plays back.
- **Steadier zoom** — pinch-to-zoom on the photo stays anchored where your fingers are, so lifting a finger to end the pinch no longer makes the image jump.

## Under the hood

- `bubbleScale` added to `Settings` (default `1`, validated/clamped to `[0.7, 1.6]`) and threaded through `useSettingsStore`. New `src/constants/plan.ts` holds `BUBBLE_SCALES` plus the pure `nearestBubbleScale`/`stepBubbleScale` helpers (unit-tested in `bubbleScale.test.ts`).
- `LimbMarker` computes dot/touch/label sizes from a `bubbleScale` prop instead of fixed constants; `PlanCanvas` passes it through. The 44dp touch target grows to wrap dots larger than that.
- New `PlanEditBar` component replaces the canvas-overlay toolbar and the standalone `LimbSelector` bar in edit mode: it owns the bubble-size stepper, the limb row, and the group/play/sequence actions (and the safe-area bottom padding). `LimbSelector` is now a chrome-less icon row using `LIMB_ICON`/`LIMB_ICON_FLIP` (MaterialCommunityIcons; the foot glyph is mirrored for the left foot).
- `PlaybackControls` bottom padding changed to `insets.bottom + SPACING.lg`.
- `settingsStorage` tests updated for the new field; the pinch-zoom focal fix (#128 follow-up) landed earlier on this branch.
