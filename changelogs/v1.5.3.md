# v1.5.3

Plan a route straight from the main photo, log grades in YDS or French, duplicate a plan to try variations, and place overlapping moves without fighting the old ones.

## What's new

**Route planner**
- **Plan route button** — the add/edit climb form now has a **Plan route** button sitting between **Cancel** and **Save**. Tap it to jump straight into the planner on your main photo; the finished plan shows up as a new note on the climb, where you can add text to it later.
- **Overlapping moves are easier to place** — tapping near an earlier move on the wall now places a new move there instead of selecting the old one. Only your current hand/foot positions stay tappable; superseded moves sit quietly underneath. Edit or reorder past moves from the move list as before.
- **Two-digit move numbers** — the sequence badge no longer clips, so moves 10, 11, 12 … read correctly.
- **Duplicate a plan** — tapping a note with a plan now offers **Duplicate plan**, which copies the photo and its moves into a new note so you can tweak a variation without losing the original.

**Grades**
- **YDS and French grading systems** — a new **Grades** setting lets you log climbs in the **V-scale** (default), **YDS** (5.10a…), or **French** (6a+…) system. Whichever you pick becomes the scale in the grade picker and the History grade filter. Existing climbs keep the grade they were logged with.

## Under the hood

- New `GradeSystem` type and a `GRADE_SYSTEMS` registry (`constants/grades`) holding ordered token lists for V/YDS/French. `gradeUtils` gained `isKnownGrade`, `gradeSystemOf`, `gradeRungs`, and `gradeRungIndex`; `gradeSortValue`/`isValidGradeOrRange` now recognize all three systems via a single ascending master index, while the V-only `parseGrade`/`parseGradeRange`/range mode are unchanged. `historyFilters` filters on the generalized rung index; `HistoryFilterBar` and `GradePicker` read the active system (YDS/French render a single-select scale; V keeps bases + modifier + range). Persisted as a new `gradeSystem` setting (default `V`).
- `LimbMarker` takes an `interactive` prop; `PlanCanvas` marks superseded (muted) markers non-interactive (`pointerEvents="none"`) and excludes them from the placement guard, so taps fall through to place a new move. The sequence badge uses `numberOfLines={1}` and grows to fit two digits.
- `RouteForm` gained a **Plan route** action (`handlePlanRoute`) that persists the draft, appends a photo note anchored to the cover shot, and opens the planner on it — shown only when draft persistence is wired (`onPersistDraft`).
- New `duplicateNotePlan` query deep-copies a note + its plan/moves in one transaction; exposed via `usePlanStore.duplicatePlan` and wired through `NoteActionSheet` → `RouteCard`.
