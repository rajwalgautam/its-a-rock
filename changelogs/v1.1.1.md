# v1.1.1

A small polish release fixing keyboard interactions, improving visual hierarchy, and remembering your last-used climbing location.

## What's new

**Keyboard & form improvements**
- **Notes field no longer cut off by keyboard** — the form now leaves plenty of space below the notes field so you can see what you're typing, and the Save/Cancel buttons stay accessible even with a full-screen keyboard.
- **Remember your last location** — when adding a new climb, the location field now pre-fills with the last gym or location you logged a climb at, saving you typing the same gym name repeatedly.

**UI polish**
- **"This week" and "All Climbs" titles are larger** — easier to scan the headers of each tab at a glance.
- **Video icon on tiles** — tiles now show a small video icon in the top-left corner if that climb has video attached, so you can spot recorded sends at a glance.
- **Delete confirmation modal is centered** — the "Delete this climb?" confirmation now appears in the center of the screen instead of the top, so it's harder to accidentally dismiss.

**Video playback**
- **Videos mute by default** — videos play silently in the gallery viewer (you can unmute with native controls), so climbing videos won't blast sound when you're reviewing a send.

## Under the hood

- Added `lastLocationName` to the settings store and `AsyncStorage` so the form can pre-fill with the last-entered gym name on creation.
- `RouteForm` now calls `setLastLocationName` on successful save, capturing the location for the next climb.
- `MediaViewer`'s `VideoPage` now sets `player.muted = true` on initialization.
- Increased `scrollContent.paddingBottom` to give more breathing room below the notes field before the pinned footer.
- Added `videoBadge` style to `RouteTile` and conditional rendering of the video-camera icon (top-left) when `media.some(m => m.type === 'video')`.
- Delete confirmation modal (`RouteContextMenu`) now renders inside a centered container for better UX.
- Increased title font size to `FONT_SIZE.xxl` (28px) for "This week" and "All Climbs" headers.
